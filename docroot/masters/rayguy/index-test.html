Pro Football Hall of Fame

Ray Guy - Class of 2014

Ray Guy is considered the greatest punter ever. He famously boomed towering punts into the air and helped usher in the term “hang time.” After seven rejections by Pro Football Hall of Fame voters, the Raider who wore No. 8 finally got the call in February. On Saturday, August 2, 2014, the former Thomson and Southern Mississippi star will become the first true punter inducted into the hall in Canton, Ohio.
