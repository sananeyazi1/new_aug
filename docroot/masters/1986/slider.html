How Jack Nicklaus played the final 9 holes at the 1986 Masters
(best viewed on desktop and tablet)
  • No. 10
    How Nicklaus played it
    • Driver to right side of fairway
    • 4-iron to green
    • 25-foot putt for birdie

    "I had about a 25-foot putt, knocked that putt in, big sort of rainbow, right to left, and that was pretty good."

    Par 4, 485 yards
  • No. 11
    How Nicklaus played it
    • Driver to right center of fairway
    • 8-iron to green
    • 25-foot putt for birdie

    "Whoops, that's pretty good. I've made three in a row here now."

    Par 4, 455 yards
  • No. 12
    How Nicklaus played it
    • 7-iron over left side of green
    • Chip to 6 feet
    • Two-putts for bogey

    "I said, 'OK, let's don't do something stupid at 12.'"

    Par 3, 155 yards
  • No. 13
    How Nicklaus played it
    • 3-wood to left side of fairway
    • 3-iron to green
    • Two-putts from 30 feet for birdie

    "I'm still a long way back.'"

    Par 5, 465 yards
  • No. 14
    How Nicklaus played it
    • 3-wood to right center of fairway
    • 6-iron to back fringe
    • Chip to 1 foot, made par
    "At 14, I hit my second shot and it just went through the green. I had a little chip shot that I played and ran it down like that, and made my par."
    Par 4, 405 yards
  • No. 15
    How Nicklaus played it
    • Driver to center of fairway
    • 4-iron to green
    • 12-foot putt for eagle

    "I turned to Jackie and I had 212 yards, I believe, and I said, 'How far do you think a 3 will go here? And I don't mean a club.'"
    Par 5, 500 yards
  • No. 16
    How Nicklaus played it
    • 5-iron to green
    • 3-foot putt for birdie

    "The reception at 16 was probably - I've had a lot of great receptions at 16 in my life, but that one was pretty special."

    Par 3, 170 yards
  • No. 17
    How Nicklaus played it
    • Driver to left rough
    • Pitching wedge to green
    • 11-foot putt for birdie

    "The ball went in and I guess that was the first time I was leading. I didn't know that. All I knew was I was in pretty good shape."

    Par 4, 400 yards
  • No. 18
    How Nicklaus played it
    • 3-wood to center of fairway
    • 5-iron to green
    • Two-putts from 40 feet for par

    "I kind of enjoyed the last 10 holes."
    Par 4, 405 yards
