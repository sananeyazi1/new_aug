How to use this app

All the standard iPad gestures will work in this app. We've also added several "+" features such as individual story views, photo galleries, multimedia editorial and interactive advertising.

  • Swipe your fingers across the screen to turn pages (as if reading a book).
  • Use the pinch gesture to reduce the size of the content on the screen.
  • Use the reverse pinch gesture to increase the size of the content on the screen.
  • Tap on an article to go to a story view. It allows you to view an individual article in a full-page view.
  • Tap on article continue-lines or teasers on section fronts to jump to the associated page.
  • Multimedia: If a triangle play button appears on a photo, tap it to watch. You must be connected to the Internet to view video.
  • Photo galleries: Tap a photo to open a larger version of the image. If there are multiple images associated with that photo and story (not all will be displayed on the page), a number of corresponding dots will be displayed on the bottom of the photo. Just swipe to view additional photos.
